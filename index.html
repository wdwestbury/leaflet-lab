Recession & Recovery

From December 2007 to 2009, the U.S . entered into one of the worst recessions since the great depression, now known as the Great Recession. Unemployment rose throughout the United States and recovery was slow across many sectors. This map shows recovery by industry for 13 industry sectors across 98 U.S. metro areas.
